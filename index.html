Every Type of Property Damage Handled by Fanno Beaver Restoration — Beaverton OR

Whether you are facing a minor appliance leak in Cedar Mill or a major sewage backup in a King City commercial building — our complete restoration service menu has you covered from first response through final rebuild.

Emergency Water Damage Restoration

Burst supply lines, storm flooding, overflowing appliances, and roof intrusions all send water racing through floors, walls, and ceilings at alarming speed. Our extraction crews deploy across Beaverton's neighborhoods — from Sexton Mountain to West Slope — with truck-mounted vacuum units, industrial dehumidifiers, and axial air movers designed to remove water fast and dry structures completely. We track every moisture reading until IICRC S500 dry standards are confirmed throughout the entire structure.

Full Emergency Restoration Details

Water Damage Repair & Full Reconstruction

Drying alone does not restore a water-damaged home. Buckled subfloors, stained ceilings, crumbling drywall, and warped trim boards all need expert hands. Our in-house construction crew — covering carpentry, drywall installation, interior painting, and flooring — rebuilds every damaged room so the finished result is completely indistinguishable from the original. We work within your insurance scope to keep the rebuild on budget and on schedule for properties throughout Sherwood, Lake Oswego, and all surrounding communities.

Basement Flood Cleanup & Sewage Extraction

Beaverton's wet winters and clay-heavy soil create conditions where basement flooding is not just possible — it is predictable. Our basement flood team responds to clean water intrusion, gray water overflow, and Category 3 sewage backups with full containment, extraction, antimicrobial treatment, and structural drying protocols. We then help property owners in Aloha, Tualatin, and across the Tualatin Valley implement prevention strategies so the same disaster does not happen twice.

Full Basement Cleanup Details

Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration

Even a small kitchen fire leaves acidic soot residue that etches into countertops, walls, and appliances within hours. Smoke penetrates insulation, HVAC ducts, and soft furnishings — spreading odor throughout your entire home long after the fire is out. Our FSRT-certified specialists deodorize at the molecular level using thermal fogging and hydroxyl generators, remove all soot and char, stabilize compromised structures, and rebuild fire-damaged rooms to original condition. We coordinate all insurance documentation simultaneously to minimize your claim timeline.

Full Fire Restoration Details

Mold Remediation & Indoor Air Quality Restoration

Hidden mold colonies behind drywall, under carpet padding, and inside ceiling cavities are extremely common in the Pacific Northwest — and extremely dangerous to ignore. Our AMRT-certified team conducts thorough air and surface sampling to map the full extent of contamination, builds negative-pressure containment barriers, removes all compromised materials, and treats every remaining surface with EPA-registered fungicide. Post-clearance air quality testing confirms that spore counts have returned to safe, natural outdoor levels before we lift containment and return your property. We serve homeowners near Fanno Creek Trail, Cooper Mountain Nature Park, and throughout all of Portland.
